Key Personnel

Pradeep J. Perera
MSCE, PE (President)

Prad is a Professional Engineer in Maryland, Virginia and DC with over 35 years of professional experience. His technical expertise is primarily in geotechnical engineering, materials engineering, and in-situ testing. He holds a number of national awards for his program management.

Prad holds a Master’s Degree in Civil Engineering from West Virginia University and a Bachelor’s Degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Nottingham.

Edward Dalton
MSCE, PE (Cheif Operating Officer)

Ed has more than 35 years of experience in Geotechnical Engineering and Construction Management in the Mid-Atlantic region, further strengthening SaLUT’s ability to provide exceptional service to our clients. Ed oversees SaLUT’s geotechnical engineering and drilling services program.

Ed received his BSCE and MSCE in Soil Mechanics from the University of Maryland College Park and is a registered professional engineer in Maryland and Delaware.

Imran M. Syed
PhD, PE (Project Manager)

Imran is responsible for management, execution and supervision of geotechnical, pavements and materials engineering projects. He has over 20 years’ experience in the fields of geotechnical, pavements and construction materials engineering. His experience encompasses both research and practical applications.

Imran received his Master’s and PhD from Texas A&M University.

 

 

Aung Banyar
PhD, PE (Geotechnical Engineer)

Banyar is responsible for providing technical support to geotechnical investigations, environmental site assessments, and technical expertise on geotechnical issues pertaining to projects SaLUT is engaged on.   He supervises and manages engineering technicians in accordance with company policies and applicable laws. He has over 15 years’ experience in the field of geotechnical engineering.

Banyar received his PhD in Civil Engineering from the University of Tokyo and is a registered professional engineer in the state of Virginia.

Shannon Hudgins
PE (Project Manager)

Shannon has been involved in geotechnical engineering and construction material testing and observation services for over 15 years. He has experience in geotechnical design of shallow and deep foundations, soil improvement design methods, pavement design, slope stability analysis, stormwater management evaluations, and forensic evaluations of distressed pavements and building components. He is a professional engineer registered in Maryland.
